FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

When will I receive my license key?
+
After your PayPal payment is confirmed, we'll send your license key to your registered email address within 24 hours.
What happens if I get a new Mac?
+
Personal licenses are tied to one Mac. If you get a new Mac, contact support (support@mojosoft.biz) and we'll reissue a license key for your new Mac. Business licenses work on unlimited Macs, so no reissue is needed.
Can I get a refund?
+
We generally do not offer refunds after purchase. We recommend using the 7-day free trial to fully test all features before buying.
Can I get a receipt?
+
Yes, email us at support@mojosoft.biz and we'll issue a receipt. Let us know if you need any specific details like company name or address.
